Turkey Chili

Description

One of the best things about this simple turkey chili is just that… it is simple. Most of the ingredients you have in your pantry at all times.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil and onion on sauté function in Instant Pot. Cook until softened.
  2. Add add spices and turkey. Sauté until turkey is browned.
