Turkey Chili

Description

One of the best things about this simple turkey chili is just that… it is simple. Most of the ingredients you have in your pantry at all times.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbps olive oil
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1.5 tbsp chili powder
  • 1.5 tsp cumin
  • 1 can (28 oz) can crushed tomato
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat oil and add onion to sauté. Cook until softened.
  2. Add the turkey and spices. Sauté until turkey is browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes and broth. Close lid and cook high for 10 min.
